The Basics

What Is Pickleball?

Pickleball is a paddle sport that blends tennis, badminton, and ping-pong. You play on a smaller court with a solid paddle and a light, perforated plastic ball.

It's easy to learn, easy on the body, and seriously social — most people are rallying within their first session. That's exactly why it's the fastest-growing sport in the country, and why Miami can't get enough of it.

Find Your Level

What Level Am I?

Not sure where you fit? Here's a quick guide — and our coaches will help you find your real level fast.

2.0–2.5

Beginner

Brand new to the game. Learning the rules, scoring, the kitchen, and basic shots.

3.0

Lower Intermediate

Consistent serves and returns. Getting comfortable with dinking and staying out of the kitchen.

3.5

Intermediate

Reliable shots, third-shot drops, and the start of real strategy and shot selection.

4.0

Advanced

Strong all-around game — positioning, resets, and smart point construction.

4.5+

Competitive

Tournament-level play — speedups, counters, hands battles, and advanced strategy.
