What is Dink Club?

More Than a Court.
It's the Club.

Dink Club is an outdoor pickleball facility in Miami, FL, with 6 professional courts at 10855 SW 112nd Ave. We offer open play, court reservations, private lessons, clinics, leagues, and tournaments — plus a pro shop stocked with CRBN paddles, apparel, and accessories.

Whether you're picking up a paddle for the first time or chasing a tournament title, you've got a home here. Open 8am–10pm, Monday through Saturday, by reservation.
