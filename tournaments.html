Why Compete Here

Real Brackets.
Real Community.

Dink Club runs competitive pickleball the way it should be — well-organized brackets, fair play, and the kind of crowd that cheers for everybody. Whether you're chasing your first medal or defending bragging rights, there's a division for you.

All play happens on our six outdoor courts at 10855 SW 112nd Ave in Kendall, with refreshments and restrooms on site and free parking — so players and spectators can make a day of it.
