Learn to Play

Get on the Court
with Confidence

Pickleball is the easiest sport to start and the hardest to put down — and Dink Club is the best place in Miami to learn it. Whether you've never held a paddle or you're chasing a higher DUPR rating, our coaches meet you where you are and build a clear path to better play.

Every lesson happens on our outdoor courts at 10855 SW 112nd Ave in Kendall, tucked behind the JCC next to the baseball fields. Pick a one-on-one private lesson for focused, fast progress, or join a group clinic to learn alongside players at your level. Loaner paddles are available, so all you need is court shoes, water, and a willingness to dink responsibly.
