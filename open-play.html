How It Works

Show Up Solo.
Leave with a Crew.

Open play is the easiest way to get games without needing to bring a full group. Reserve a session, rotate in, and play with others at your level — it's how most of our community first connects.

Whether you're brand new or already competing, open play gives you quality games, familiar faces, and that unmistakable Dink Club energy. All play is by reservation, so grab your spot online before you head over.
