Who We Are

More Than a Court.
It's the Club.

Dink Club is an outdoor pickleball facility in Miami, FL, with 6 courts at 10855 SW 112nd Ave in Kendall. We built Dink Club to be the spot where Miami plays pickleball — whether you're picking up a paddle for the first time or chasing a higher DUPR rating.

Open play, court reservations, private lessons, clinics, leagues, and tournaments all happen here, plus a pro shop stocked with CRBN paddles, apparel, and accessories. Tucked behind the JCC next to the baseball fields, with free parking on site.
