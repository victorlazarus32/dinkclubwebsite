Who We Are

More Than a Game.
It's a Lifestyle.

At Dink Club, pickleball is more than a game — it's a lifestyle.

Nestled in the heart of Kendall, Miami, Dink Club is an outdoor pickleball facility with 6 courts at 10855 SW 112nd Ave, founded to create a premier pickleball destination where sport, community, wellness, and social connection come together. Designed for players who want more than just court time, we deliver an experience that's energetic, welcoming, and uniquely South Florida.

Our facility is home to a thriving community of players — from beginners discovering the sport for the first time to highly competitive athletes chasing their next challenge. Whether you're joining an open play session, competing in a signature tournament, playing in a league, or enjoying a social event, every visit is an opportunity to connect, compete, and belong.

The Atmosphere

Where the Community Comes Together

What truly makes Dink Club special is the atmosphere. We've cultivated a vibrant community where friendships are formed, healthy lifestyles are embraced, and players feel inspired to improve both on and off the court. Our events are thoughtfully designed to bring people together, creating memorable experiences that extend far beyond the final point.

Year-Round

A Dynamic Calendar

From sunset matches and social mixers to DUPR competitions, Moneyball events, leagues, clinics, and championship tournaments, Dink Club offers a dynamic calendar that keeps players engaged year-round. Every event reflects our commitment to excellence, fun, and a sense of community that keeps players coming back.

As pickleball continues to grow, so does our vision — an exceptional playing environment, innovative programming, and a welcoming culture that make Dink Club one of South Florida's most exciting pickleball communities. Whether you're here to compete, improve your game, meet new friends, or simply enjoy the energy of the sport, Dink Club is your place to play.
