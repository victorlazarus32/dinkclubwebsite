Court Time

Your Court, Whenever You Want It

Looking for pickleball courts in Miami? Dink Club has 6 outdoor courts ready to book Monday through Sunday, 8am–10pm. Reserve a court for a quick game with friends, a practice session, or a full afternoon of play.

All play is by reservation — no guessing whether a court is open. Pick your time online, show up, and play. We're at 10855 SW 112nd Ave, tucked behind the JCC next to the baseball fields, with free parking on site.
