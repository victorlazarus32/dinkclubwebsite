What Is DUPR?

DUPR — the Dynamic Universal Pickleball Rating — is the number that tells you (and us) your true skill level, so you land in the right tournament bracket and get competitive games.

The Basics

One Number, Everywhere You Play

DUPR stands for Dynamic Universal Pickleball Rating. It's a single, global rating that scores every player on the same scale — from 2.0 (just starting) to 8.0 (touring pro) — regardless of age, gender, or where you play. Most recreational and competitive players land between 2.5 and 5.5.

Unlike a self-rating (where players guess their own level), DUPR is built from your actual match results. That makes it honest and consistent — a 3.5 in Miami is the same as a 3.5 anywhere else, which is exactly what makes fair brackets possible.

The Scale

Reading the 2.0–8.0 Range

A quick guide to what each band of the DUPR scale generally means on court.

2.0–2.9

New / Beginner

Learning the rules, scoring, and consistent contact. Building the basics.

3.0–3.9

Intermediate

Reliable rallies, improving strategy, third-shot drops, and court positioning.

4.0–4.9

Advanced

Strong shot control, tactics, speed-ups, and consistent competitive play.

5.0–8.0

Elite / Pro

High-level and professional players competing in top brackets and pro events.

Dink Club tournament divisions run 2.5–5.0+, so there's a bracket for nearly every level.

How It Works

How Your Rating Is Calculated

DUPR updates automatically from your recorded matches — here's what moves the number.

①

Match Results

Wins and losses against other rated players — the core of your rating.

②

Score Margin

How close the games were — a tight loss to a strong team still counts.

③

Format

Singles, doubles, and mixed doubles are each weighed into your rating.

④

Recency

Recent matches carry more weight; ~20 matches a year keeps it reliable.

Why It Matters at Dink Club

Fair Brackets, Better Games

When you sign up for a Dink Club tournament or bracketed event, your DUPR helps us place you in the right division — so you're matched against players at your level instead of getting blown out or steamrolling the field.

Don't have a DUPR yet? No problem. Open play and court rentals are open to everyone, and playing recorded matches and events at Dink Club is a great way to build your rating.
