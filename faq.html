Visiting & Booking

Dink Club is at 10855 SW 112nd Ave, Miami, FL 33176, in Kendall — tucked behind the JCC next to the baseball fields, with free parking on site. We have 6 outdoor pickleball courts.

We're open Monday through Saturday, 8am–10pm, by reservation. We're closed Sundays except for privately booked events.

All play is by reservation — walk-ins aren't available. Reserve a court or an open-play session online or by phone before you come.

Book online through our CourtReserve booking page or call 305-209-3133. You can reserve private courts, open-play sessions, lessons, and clinics.
