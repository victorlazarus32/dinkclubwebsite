Learn in a Group

Drill, Play,
Level Up

Clinics are the fastest, most fun way to improve in Miami. You'll work through focused drills, learn real game strategy, and put it straight into live play — all alongside players at your level, guided by Dink Club's experienced coaches.

Held on our outdoor courts at 10855 SW 112nd Ave in Kendall (behind the JCC, next to the baseball fields), clinics run Monday through Saturday. Loaner paddles are available, so bring court shoes, water, and a good attitude.
