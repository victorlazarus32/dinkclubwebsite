The DCPL

Play a Full Season,
Not Just One Day

The Dink Club Pickleball League (DCPL) is our recurring league for players who want more than a one-off tournament. Join a season of weekly matches, climb the standings, and play alongside a community that shows up week after week.

Every match is DUPR-rated, so your results count toward your official rating while you compete. Divisions span skill levels, so whether you're brand new to league play or chasing the top of the table, there's a place for you on our six outdoor courts in Kendall.

Good to Know

League FAQs

DCPL is Dink Club's recurring pickleball league in Miami — weekly matches, live standings, and team play across a full season. New seasons are announced on Swish and our Instagram.

Divisions run from beginner-friendly through advanced, so you're matched with players in your range. Match results are DUPR-rated.

League seasons are registered through Swish. Open the current season and sign up online, or call 305-813-4238 with questions.

It depends on the season format — some are team-based, others individual with rotating partners. Need help getting placed? Call 305-813-4238 and we'll sort it out.
