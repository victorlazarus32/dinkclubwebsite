Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Dink Club ("Dink Club," "we," "us," or "our") operates the pickleball facility at 10855 SW 112nd Ave, Miami, FL 33176 and this website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or contacting us, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact details — your name, email address, and phone number when you fill out our contact or sign-up forms, or when you call or message us.
  • Booking information — details needed to reserve courts, lessons, clinics, or events (handled primarily through our third-party booking partners, below).
  • Messages — any information you include when you contact us.

We may also automatically collect limited technical information (such as your device type, browser, and general usage) through standard website analytics and hosting logs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request.
  • To manage court reservations, lessons, clinics, events, and league play.
  • To send you information about your bookings and, where permitted, updates about Dink Club programs and events.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and services.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to run our business. When you use certain features, your information may be processed by these services under their own privacy policies:

  • CourtReserve / Swish — court and event booking and payments.
  • Google Maps — to display our location and directions.
  • Netlify — website hosting.
  • Instagram / Meta — if you follow or interact with our social media.

We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies

Our site and its embedded services (such as Google Maps) may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences and understand site usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect some features.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to stop sending you promotional messages, by contacting us using the details below. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. Bookings for minors are made by a parent or guardian.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
